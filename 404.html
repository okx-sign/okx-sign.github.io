Terms of Agreement NDA
01

Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means information disclosed by the Disclosing Party in any form in connection with discussions of a potential cooperation (the "Project"), including without limitation:

  1. the existence, objectives, scope, business model, and implementation plan of the Project;
  2. the fact that the Parties are in discussions regarding the Project;
  3. the Disclosing Party's technical solutions, business strategies, product plans, financial data, user information, and counterparty information;
  4. any information marked "Confidential" or reasonably understood to be confidential by its nature.
02

Obligations

The Receiving Party shall:

  1. hold Confidential Information in strict confidence, using at least the same standard of care as it uses to protect its own confidential information of similar importance, but acting no less than reasonably prudently;
  2. use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of evaluating and discussing the Project;
  3. not disclose Confidential Information to any third party; and
  4. disclose Confidential Information only on a strict need-to-know basis to its employees, Affiliates, and professional advisors (including legal, accounting, and financial advisors) who are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.
03

Exceptions

The obligations under this Agreement do not apply to information that:

  1. is publicly known at the time of disclosure or subsequently becomes publicly known through no fault of the Receiving Party;
  2. was lawfully in the Receiving Party's possession prior to disclosure, free of any confidentiality obligation;
  3. is lawfully received from a third party having the right to disclose;
  4. was independently developed by the Receiving Party; or
  5. is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or order of a governmental authority (provided the Receiving Party, to the extent legally permissible, gives the Disclosing Party prior notice).
04

Term

This Agreement takes effect upon execution by the Receiving Party, and the confidentiality obligations remain in force for twelve (12) months thereafter.

05

Destruction

Upon the Disclosing Party's written request or termination of discussions regarding the Project, the Receiving Party shall, within ten (10) business days, destroy all Confidential Information in its possession (including copies, electronic versions, and derivative materials) and confirm such destruction in writing. Copies retained as required by law, or retained on automated backup systems where individual deletion is not reasonably practicable, shall remain subject to this Agreement.

06

No Grant of Rights

This Agreement does not grant the Receiving Party any intellectual property, license, or other right in or to the Confidential Information. This Agreement does not obligate either Party to continue discussions or to enter into any definitive agreement.

07

Remedies

In the event of a breach, the Receiving Party shall immediately cease the breach and indemnify the Disclosing Party for actual losses suffered (including reasonable legal fees). Given that monetary damages may not adequately compensate for the harm caused by a breach, the Disclosing Party may seek injunctive relief from any court or arbitral tribunal of competent jurisdiction without the need to post a bond.

08

Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Singapore. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be finally resolved by arbitration administered by the Singapore International Arbitration Centre (SIAC) in accordance with its Arbitration Rules then in force. The seat of arbitration shall be Singapore, the language of the arbitration shall be English, and the tribunal shall consist of one (1) arbitrator.

09

Miscellaneous

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement of the Parties with respect to confidentiality for the Project. Electronic execution shall have the same legal effect as paper execution. This Agreement is made in English and Chinese; in case of inconsistency, the English version shall prevail.

01

保密信息

"保密信息"指在双方就一项潜在合作（"本项目"）进行讨论期间，披露方以任何形式向接收方披露的信息，包括但不限于：

  1. 本项目的存在、目标、范围、商业模式及实施计划；
  2. 双方就本项目进行洽谈的事实本身；
  3. 披露方的技术方案、业务策略、产品规划、财务数据、用户信息、合作方信息；
  4. 任何标注为"保密"或依其性质合理可知为保密的信息。
02

保密义务

接收方应：

  1. 对保密信息严格保密，所采取的保密措施不得低于其保护自身同等重要信息时所采取的合理审慎标准；
  2. 仅将保密信息用于评估、洽谈本项目；
  3. 不向任何第三方披露保密信息；
  4. 仅在严格必要知悉原则下，向因评估本项目确需了解保密信息的员工、关联方及承担同等保密义务的专业顾问（包括律师、会计师、财务顾问）披露。
03

例外情形

下列情形不构成违反本协议：

  1. 在披露时已为公众所知悉，或在披露后非因接收方过错而为公众所知悉的信息；
  2. 接收方在披露前已合法持有且不负保密义务的信息；
  3. 接收方从有权披露的第三方处合法获得的信息；
  4. 接收方独立开发取得的信息；
  5. 因法律、监管机关或政府机关的强制要求而披露的信息（接收方应在法律允许范围内事先通知披露方）。
04

期限

本协议自接收方签署之日起生效，保密义务的有效期为 十二（12）个月。

05

信息销毁

在披露方书面要求或本项目讨论终止后，接收方应在 十（10）个工作日 内销毁所持有的全部保密信息（含复印件、电子副本及衍生材料），并书面确认销毁完毕。因法律要求而保留，或留存于自动备份系统中难以单独删除的副本，仍受本协议约束。

06

不授予权利

本协议不向接收方授予披露方所拥有的任何知识产权、许可或其他权利。本协议亦不构成任何一方继续洽谈或签订后续正式合作协议的义务。

07

违约责任

接收方违反本协议的，应立即停止违约行为，并赔偿披露方因此遭受的实际损失（包括合理的律师费用）。鉴于违约所造成的损害可能难以仅通过金钱赔偿予以充分弥补，披露方有权向有管辖权的法院或仲裁庭申请禁令救济，且无需提供担保。

08

适用法律与争议解决

本协议适用 新加坡 法律。因本协议引起的或与本协议相关的任何争议，应提交 新加坡国际仲裁中心（SIAC） 按其届时有效的仲裁规则进行最终仲裁。仲裁地为 新加坡，仲裁语言为 英文，由 一（1）名 仲裁员审理。

09

其他

本协议构成双方就本项目保密事宜达成的完整协议。本协议的电子签署与纸质签署具有同等法律效力。本协议以中、英两种语言订立，如中英文版本存在不一致之处，应以英文版本为准。
